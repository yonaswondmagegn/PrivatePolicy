This privacy policy describes how your data is collected, used, and protected when you use our Chrome extension (referred to as “the Extension”) designed to automatically remove browsing history related to adult or unwanted websites. 1. Information We Collect The Extension does not collect or transmit any personal information to any server or third party. All data used by the Extension is stored locally in your browser and is used solely for the functionality of the Extension. Specifically: Browsing History: Accessed only to identify and delete visits to blocked or flagged websites. Tabs & Sessions: Used to monitor real-time activity and remove browsing history only after the tab or window is closed. Storage: Used to store user preferences, including custom blocked sites and any removed URLs, locally in your browser. 2. How We Use Your Information All information accessed by the Extension is used locally and automatically to: Detect visits to blocked or adult content websites. Delete such entries from your browsing history. Store your preferences like custom sites to block or allow. We do not send, share, or sell any data to third parties. 3. Permissions To perform its intended function, the Extension requires the following permissions: history: To delete visited URLs from browser history. tabs: To monitor open and updated tabs for unwanted content. storage: To save user-defined custom site lists and history tracking. webNavigation: To detect when a site has fully loaded for history evaluation. sessions: To manage cleanup after tabs or windows are closed. These permissions are strictly used for functionality and not for tracking. 4. Data Sharing We do not share any user data with anyone. 5. Data Security Since no personal data is collected or stored externally, there is no risk of exposure through this Extension. All processing happens on your local device. 6. Changes to This Policy We may update this privacy policy from time to time. You will be notified of any major changes through the Chrome Web Store listing.
